Built to listen like a producer.

Most music AI products chase generation. Fonna is about decision-making. It helps artists hear what is working, what is holding things back, and what to change first.

Focused critique

Fonna stays on the parts of the record that shape the listening experience most directly: arrangement, production, and mix.

Clear next actions

No padded language. No abstract theory. Every report is meant to help you move the track forward in the next session.

Designed for works in progress

Use it on demos, near-finishes, or release candidates. Fonna is for the stage where choices still matter.

How it works

Simple on the surface. Serious under the hood.

1

Upload your track

Send Fonna the song you are working on and add a short note if you want to steer the feedback.

2

Fonna listens in producer passes

It listens for momentum, section contrast, texture choices, vocal placement, low-end clarity, and mix hierarchy.

3

Get the report

Receive a concise producer report with a snapshot, domain notes, best next move, and priority actions.

What makes it different

Fonna is not trying to replace taste. It is trying to sharpen decisions.

No generic praise

The goal is not to flatter the track. The goal is to help you understand how the record is landing and what is getting in its way.

No songwriting detours

Fonna stays disciplined. It does not drift into lyric critique or vague genre commentary when the real issue is production or mix.

Built for repeat use

Come back after every revision. The strongest use case is hearing whether your latest decisions actually improved the record.
